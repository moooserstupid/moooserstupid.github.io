Lightplay
An atmospheric puzzle game about bringing light to a dark world.
Project Details / Background
LightPlay is a game I made in less than a week for the 2023 Winter Gamejam hosted by Mindstorm Studios. It involves redirecting a source of light using various light manipulation mechanics and your ingenuity to spread light and capture as many gems as possible. It has a minimalist and low-poly aesthetic with a soothing soft pastel colour palette. The game was developed in C# using Unity. The assets are all public-domain.
